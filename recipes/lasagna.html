Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir onion, carrot, shallot, and prosciutto until onion is translucent and prosciutto releases some fat, about 10 minutes
  2. Pour red wine over meat mixture; increase heat and cook until wine evaporates, about 3 minutes
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat; add flour. Whisk vigorously until mixture is smooth.
