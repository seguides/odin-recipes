Lobster

Description

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces lobster tail, cleaned and chopped
  • 4 (8 ounce) filet mignon (beef tenderloin)
  • 4 slices bacon

Steps

  1. Sprinkle tenderloins all over with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. While tenderloins are cooking, melt 1/4 cup of butter over medium heat
  3. While the lobster is in the oven, heat the remaining 1/4 cup of butter
